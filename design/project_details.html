Project location: Jakarta

I was at the station on my way home when I overheard the conversation between the manager of the City Station and a person that seems to be an electrician. The manager was quite upset because of the operational cost of the lighting there, which was suprisingly expensive. Hearing that, I suddenly remembered that there is a technology that couldsolve that problem. A technology called LED lamp, which uses much less power than common flourescent lamp and much more sustainable too.

(Above picture) This Phillips LED light bulb only consumes 2 Watt. Ten times less power than ordinary light bulb. We need your help to raise IDR 100.000.000,- to replace the lightning in City Station to LED. In return, we have several reward we will give you as our sign of gratitude (please look at the right side of this page for details). Hopefully, this could inspire other public places to do the same. Thank you for your help!
